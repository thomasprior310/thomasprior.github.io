Hobbies & Interests

Outside of academia and work, I find joy and fulfillment in pursuing my athletic passions and indulging in captivating reads. Athletics serve as a cornerstone of my lifestyle, fostering camaraderie, discipline, and a healthy competitive spirit. From the greens of Vespra Hills Golf Club to the exhilarating fields of ultimate frisbee, rugby, and volleyball, I thrive in the energy of team sports and the solitary challenge of individual endeavors like weightlifting. In the realm of literature, I'm drawn to a diverse range of genres, with a particular fascination for thought-provoking narratives. Exploring dystopian landscapes and the intricacies of mathematics, I've been captivated by titles such as "Weapons of Math Destruction", "Time Shelter," and "The Man in the High Castle." These works not only entertain but also stimulate introspection and critical thinking, aligning with my goal of continuous learning and intellectual exploration.

Volunteer Work

Giving back to my community has always been a core value, and I've been fortunate to contribute my time and efforts to various initiatives aimed at supporting those in need. One impactful experience was volunteering with the "Out of the Cold" program in downtown Barrie, where I had the opportunity to assist at a soup kitchen, providing warm meals to individuals experiencing homelessness. This experience instilled in me a deep sense of empathy and reinforced the importance of compassion and solidarity.During holiday seasons, I dedicated my time to volunteering at the Barrie Food Bank, recognizing the heightened need for support during these times of celebration and reflection. I found fulfillment in helping to organize and distribute essential resources to families facing food insecurity, embodying the spirit of giving and community support. In Grade 12, I took on a leadership role by spearheading the Thanksgiving food drive at my high school. Through meticulous planning and enthusiastic engagement, I rallied my peers to donate generously, amplifying our impact on local food security initiatives. Additionally, I organized a successful buyout event, where proceeds were directed towards the Barrie Food Bank, further amplifying our collective contribution to addressing hunger in our community. These experiences have shaped my understanding of social responsibility and reinforced my commitment to making a positive difference in the lives of others.
